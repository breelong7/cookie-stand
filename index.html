Pat's Salmon Cookies

Are you getting tired of the same old muffins, cakes, and scones to pair up with your beloved cup of coffee or tea? Well the answer is finally here - salmon cookies! They're cookies that are carefully crafted into the shape of a salmon, with real salmon used in the recipe! Stop into one of our five convenient locations listed in the Locations tab to try this popular new treat, and even take some home to share with friends and family!
